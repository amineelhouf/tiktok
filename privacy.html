Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Amine TikTok Automation App (“the App”) handles information authorized through TikTok OAuth.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your TikTok User ID
  • Permissions required to upload videos
  • Access tokens needed for authorized publishing

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Upload videos you approved
  • Ensure secure automation

3. Data Storage

Tokens may be stored temporarily in automation workflows. No long-term or personal storage occurs.

4. Data Sharing

Your information is never shared, sold, or transferred to third parties.

5. Revoking Access

You may revoke OAuth access at any time from TikTok account settings.
