Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Amine TikTok Automation App (“the App”) handles information when you authorize it using TikTok OAuth.

1. Information We Collect

The App only receives information that you explicitly authorize through TikTok OAuth:

  • Your TikTok User ID
  • Permissions needed to upload videos
  • Access tokens required for authorized publishing

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information only to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account
  • Upload videos you have approved
  • Ensure secure automation of the publishing workflow

3. Data Storage

Access tokens may be stored temporarily in n8n (self-hosted or cloud) to enable automation. Tokens are not shared, sold, or used for any other purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses TikTok’s official APIs and may operate through n8n automation servers. These platforms have their own privacy policies.

5. Your Rights

  • You can revoke OAuth access at any time from your TikTok settings.
  • You may request deletion of any stored tokens.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect information from individuals under TikTok’s minimum age requirement.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date.
